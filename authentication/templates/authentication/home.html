What is Dyadapt?

With Dyadapt, you will improve your reading experience. You will also have access to the Dyadapt screening test that allows you, in just 15 minutes,to detect if your child is dyslexic or not, through playing a game personalized based on 24 cognitive skills targeting both cognitive weaknesses and strengths. Additionally, Dyadapt uses advanced eye-tracking technology to help identify dyslexia by analyzing reading patterns and eye movements, providing a comprehensive and accurate screening process. We also offer a browser extension that enhances readability for dyslexics by adjusting webpage content for a better browsing experience.
